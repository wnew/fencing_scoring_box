The test box just plugs into a scorring box with the standard 6 wires. It
connects them up according to the weapon being used. It then shorts out or
connects the pins according to on target and off target hits. The user needs
to watch the lights and ensure that the correct lights are triggered.

W = Weapon Pin
L = Lame Pin
G = Ground

Wa - Lb
Wb - La
Wa - Gb
Wb - Ga
La - Gb
Lb - Ga
