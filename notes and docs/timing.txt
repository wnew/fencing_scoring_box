Foil
=====
Depress 14ms (+/-1ms)
Lockout 300ms (+/-25ms tolerance)
No touch is registered on the piste


Epee
=====
Register when the circuit is closed.
Lockout 45ms (+/-5ms).
Depress >2ms
Not register a touch on the piste or gaurd


Sabre
======
Lockout 120ms +/-10ms
Depress (touch length) 0.1ms -1ms
Whipover, bloody complex!
